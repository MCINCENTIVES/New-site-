About

We believe performance is engineered, not hoped for.

MC Incentives exists to give organisations a deliberate architecture for how work is rewarded, recognised and performed — built for the way work actually operates in the AI era.

Why we exist

Most performance problems are design problems.

Organisations rarely underperform because their people lack effort. They underperform because the systems around those people — reward, recognition, performance, role design — were never built to work together.

We exist to close that gap: to replace inherited, disconnected frameworks with a deliberate system that makes performance repeatable and fair — even as work keeps changing.

Our belief

Reward is not a cost to manage. It is behavioural architecture to design.
