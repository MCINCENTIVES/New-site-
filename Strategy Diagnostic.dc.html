What it is

A performance-system diagnostic — not an HR audit.

The Strategy Diagnostic is an objective, executive-level assessment of how your reward, recognition and performance systems function as one architecture — and where they are quietly working against revenue, retention and productivity.

It exists because AI is reshaping roles faster than systems can adapt: work drifts from its definition, contribution becomes harder to measure, and misaligned reward erodes trust. We isolate the structural root causes — then sequence what to fix, and in what order.
